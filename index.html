Welcome to StampsCam!

A collection of William & Mary themed stamps and how to make them!

Here we have a collection of W&M themed stamps created for the Cypher VII hackathon. Using a 3D printer or laser wood cutter, all available in the W&M Makerspace, you can make your own stamps from this website!

Follow these steps to make your own stamps:

  1. Select a stamp from our collection.
  2. Download the STL file if you plan to 3D print your stamp or download the UFile if you plan to use a lasar cutter.
  3. Follow the remaining steps to use the machine of your choice to create your stamp. If you are a William & Mary student, check out the WM Makerspaces Website for tutorials, classes, and to make print requests.
